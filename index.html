Cheeseburber & Potato Wedges Americana

Cut Potato Wedges. Sprinkle with cooking oil. Sprinkle grated cheese on wedges just before removing from oven. Cook at 250C for 50~60 minutes.

Form ground beef into a patty and fry. Bake bread with cheese on top for 5 ~ 10 minutes. Add condiments.

Burrito Supreme

Fry garlic and onions in cooking oil with 1 tsp of chili powder. Add red kidney beans and water. Boil until beans are soft. Mash beans and mix with salsa.

lightly fry tortillas until warm. Wrap bean mix in tortillas

Chili Fricase

Mix water with some Mcormick chili sauce or sauce of your choosing. Brown ground beef with onions, garlic and green peppers. Mix ground beef and sauce and boil for a few minutes.

Pizza Galore

Spread sauce around pizza crust. Sprinkle grated Cheese liberally. Add toppings. Sprinkle more grated cheese. Bake at 250C for 8~10 minutes.
